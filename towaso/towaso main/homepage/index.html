Our Services

ToWaSo offers both its services and products to B to B and B to C markets.
If you are a company or municipality or any organization you will love our waste collection service.

WASTE MANAGEMENT

We are committed to environmental improvement and prevention of pollution.This include segregation, compaction of waste and transportation of waste with optimized route.

BIO-GAS

We install and help you operate the Bio-Gas plant that is easy to understand and you can easily operate it with their own benefits to suit individual needs.

VERMI COMPOST

Vermicomposting, a highly nutritious material for soil can induce 1.6 times plant growth than pesticides.it contains all the nutrients important for the proper and exponential growth of plant.

CONSULTANCY

We are providing consultancy for route optimization,garbage collection initially. Further we will extent our services to provide environmental consultancy.
